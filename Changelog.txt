## [1.2.1] - 2021-07-23

### Added
- Support for RimWorld version 1.3


## [1.2.0] - 2021-02-03

### Added
- Callouts on animal interactions - nuzzling, feeding, taming success, training success, tending and slaughtering.
- Setting to enable/disable combat callouts
- Setting to enable/disable animal interaction callouts
- Setting to enable/disable text mote queueing
- Melee manuever info available to combat callouts
- New callout entries

### Changed
- 'recipient_part' keywords changed to 'PART' in callout rules
- Affected pawn in Lethal_Hediff_Progression callout now labeled as INITIATOR instead of RECIPIENT


## [1.1.2] - 2021-01-24

### Changed
- Rearranged settings menu


## [1.1.1] - 2021-01-24

### Added
- Setting to specify minimum wound severity to throw text mote for
- Setting to allow callouts when an animal is the target (off by default)
- Setting to allow animals to call out (off by default)
- Callouts on being wounded in a situation not included by another callout
- Potentially lethal hediffs display text motes when their severity goes up
- Callouts can be triggered upon lethal hediff progression

### Changed
- Likelihood of showing callout is scaled to be proportionate to the number of available entries
- Most taunt-like callouts now have a chance to append an insult based on the caller's spicyness
- Weapon titles will be colored in callouts
- Default callout chance reduced to 15% (for new users)


## [1.1.0] - 2021-01-23

### Added
- Damaged and destroyed body parts displayed as text motes
- Text motes stay put when game paused unless generated during pause
- Label background for text motes
- Queueing of text motes to reduce overlap
- Setting to attach callouts so they stay in place above the caller
- Setting for callout frequency
- Setting to show/hide destroyed body part info
- Callouts for melee attack hitting and being hit
- Callouts for ranged attack hitting and being hit
- More callout lines
